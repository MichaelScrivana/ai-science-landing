Our Vision

Our vision is to fundamentally transform the Science department by integrating agentic artificial intelligence (AI) into our core processes, research, and operations. By leveraging agentic AI—AI systems capable of autonomous decision-making and adaptive learning—we aim to accelerate scientific discovery, enhance operational efficiency, and foster a culture of innovation. This transformation will position our department at the forefront of scientific advancement, enabling us to address complex challenges with unprecedented agility and insight.
